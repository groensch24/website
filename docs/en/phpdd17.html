Dresden's largest PHP event takes the 3rd round

We - the PHP USERGROUP DRESDEN e.V. - are proud to present the PHP Developer Day for the 3rd time in a row. Just like the past two events in 2015 and 2016 top talks, free exchange of knowledge, community and fun stand in the foreground of the event again.

Come along on SEPTEMBER 22nd at DRESDNER VOLKSHAUS, SCHÜTZENPLATZ 14 in DRESDEN, GERMANY
— Enjoy the PHP Developer Day 2017.

Alongside the 45 minute talks, we ask YOU to speak to the crowd. You'll have the opportunity to submit a lighting-talk the whole day. You'll then get your own 15 minutes slot to present your topic. We're looking forward to your contribution!

Location

The PHP Developer Day 2017 takes place at VOLKSHAUS DRESDEN am Schützenplatz 14, 01069 Dresden. The location is centrally-gated at "Bahnhof Mitte" and can easily be reached by tram, bus and suburban train.
